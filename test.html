6. KINDNESS and EMPATHY are our CORE values


Dr Kathawala grew up, surrounded by doctors in her family. Her mom is a gynecologist. At an early age, she has seen suffering and what empathy can do to patients.

Besides working here, she still spents a lot of time, helping people in need in poor neighborhoods of Jersey City NJ.

Patients at her Jersey City Clinic revere her.

She is COMMITTED to doing what's RIGHT for the patient.
