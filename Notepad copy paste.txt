<h2 style="color:black"><B> Advantages of Tooth-Colored Restorations</B></h2>
<h4><p class="text-justify">
There are many advantages to tooth-colored restorations.<br><br> <ul class="text-justify">
	<li>Composite resigns are bonded to the teeth creating a tight, superior fit to the natural tooth. <br><br>  
	<li>Chemically bonded restorations remains intact longer, are much stronger and provide a pleasing look.<br><br>
	<li>Since the resin used in tooth-colored restorations contain fluoride this can help prevent decay. <br><br>  
	<li>The resin wears like natural teeth and does not require placement at the gum line, which is healthier for your gums!
<br><br>

</ul> </p>
