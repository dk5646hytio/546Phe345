Frequently asked questions (FAQ) about Smile Makeover

Can I eat normally and will the smile makeover feel natural?

Depending on what procedures were done, a smile makeover in most instances will feel natural. For example if you have bonding done, then you can eat and chew normally. However do not bite hard or open beer bottles with your bonded teeth. If you have implants done supported by implant crown, they will feel and work like natural teeth. Though, if not properly cared for or abused by biting hard or grinding etc., even implant supported crown may get damaged overtime.

Definition of Cosmtic Terminology

Smile Shape Guide

A shape guide that helps patient to evaluate how they want to change their smile with various cosmetic procedures.

Crown Lengthening

Crown lengthening is a surgical procedure that increases the extent of a tooth structure for restorative or esthetic purposes.

Cosmetic Imaging

Cosmetic imaging allows for patients to see how the various cosmetic procedures can change their appearance, its a patient eduction tool.

Full Mouth Rehabilitation

Full mouth rehabilitation is a customized treatment that strives to optimize health and features of the entire mouth with multiple procedures.

Alveolar Bone

The alveolar bone is the bone surrounding the root of the tooth that keeps the tooth in place.

Tetracycline

Tetracycline is an antibiotic that can cause permanent gray discoloration of teeth when people use it at a young age.

Stomatitis

Stomatitis is the inflammation of the tissue that is underlying a denture that does not fit properly. It can also result from other oral health factors. Read more on Wikipedia.
